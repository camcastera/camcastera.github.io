Welcome

I am a researcher in optimization and machine learning. Since February 2022, I am a postdoc at the University of Tübingen, working on the TRINOM-DS project led by Peter Ochs and Jalal Fadili.

Prior to that, I wrote my PhD thesis at the IRIT laboratory in Toulouse (supervised by C. Févotte, E. Pauwels, J. Bolte). I was a member of the FACTORY project.

My research focuses on second-order optimization algorithms for deep learning and machine learning in general. I used to give tutorials and practicals at INP-ENSEEIHT where I taught probabilities, statistics and integration theory to master students.

Research interests

  • Optimization: non-convex, non-smooth, stochastic, etc.
  • Deep Learning
  • Theory of Machine Learning and Statistics
