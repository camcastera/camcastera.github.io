I am a CNRS PhD candidate within the SC team of IRIT, the computer science research institute of Toulouse. I will defend my Ph.D thesis in November 2021. I work under the supervision of Cédric Févotte, Édouard Pauwels and Jérôme Bolte. I am a member of the FACTORY project.

I did my master thesis under the supervision of Pierre Weiss and Frédéric de Gournay working on semi-infinite programming and inverse Problems. I graduated from INSA Toulouse and Université Paul Sabatier where I obtained master degrees respectively in "Applied Mathematics and Modeling" and "Applied Mathematics, major: Partial Differential Equations".

My research focuses on optimization algorithms for deep learning and machine learning. I also used to give tutorials and practicals at INP ENSEEIHT where I taught probabilities, statistics and integration theory to master students.

Research interests

  • Optimization: non-convex, non-smooth, stochastic, etc.
  • Deep Learning
  • Theory of Machine Learning and Statistics
